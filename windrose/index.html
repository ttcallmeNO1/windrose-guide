Your Journey Begins on a Beach

Stranded with nothing but a broken blade. Follow this guide to go from castaway to pirate legend.

💡
Choose Your Difficulty
Permanent - Choose Wisely
  • Calm Waters - Reduced enemy damage. Best for beginners.
  • High Seas - 100% balanced. The intended experience.
  • Storm's Edge - Enemies hit harder. For veterans.
  • Captain's Choice - Fully custom modifiers up to 500%.
⚠️

Cannot be changed mid-game! Pick carefully. New players start on Calm Waters.

🏝️
Islander Tutorial Quest
Complete Step-by-Step

1. Build a Bonfire

Collect 5 Wood from branches. Place it openly.

2. Build Workbench + Cooking Fire + Tent

Workbench: 5 Wood | Cooking Fire: 3 Wood + 3 Stone | Tent: 4 Wood + 10 Plant Fiber

3. Kill a Boar for Rough Hide

Two boars ambush after tent. Sows are easier than full Boars.

4. Armor Workshop + Survivor's Boots

Workshop needs 5 Wood + 10 Coarse Fabric + a roof to function.

5. Smelt Copper Ingots

Charcoal Kiln (25 Wood + 20 Clay) → Smelting Furnace (15 Clay + 30 Stone). 6 Copper Ore + 1 Charcoal = 1 Ingot. Need 2+.

6. Craft a Melee Weapon

Weaponsmith Workshop (10 Wood + 5 Copper Ingots). Craft Saber, Rapier, or Club. Pistol doesn't count!

💎

Free Boat Unlocked! After all steps, get your first boat. Quests unlock: "Rescuing the Crew" and "I Need a Bigger Boat".

20 Essential Tips
Verified by the Community
  • Craft a bag IMMEDIATELY - Torn Sailcloth Bag: 2 Coarse Fabric + 1 Rope.
  • Loot everything on the first island - Necklace of Vitality (+4 VIT), Rapier of a Thousand Cuts, Fast Travel Bell.
  • Always carry 30 Wood + 15 Fiber - Emergency Bonfire + Tent respawn points.
  • Eat two food buffs at all times - Cooked food gives stat buffs. Max 2 active.
  • Pick up beach crabs - Comfort trophies for base Rested buff.
  • Parry, don't just block - Perfect block cancels damage, saves stamina.
  • Hit once or twice, then back up - Save dash for bosses.
  • Avoid Level 3 Sows early - They can one-shot you.
  • Use fences to cheese enemies - Wildlife can't pathfind around them.
  • Middle-click = special attacks - Rapier double slash, musket precise shot.
  • Small Ficus trees = fast wood - Few hits, drop wood + fiber directly.
  • Use a shovel for plant fiber - Dig at base of clusters for more fiber.
  • Comfort Level extends Rested buff - Place furniture around base.
  • Build Disassembly Table ASAP - Break old gear into materials.
  • Carry portable bonfire + tent - Emergency respawn points.
  • Buildings can't be moved - Demolish to refund, then rebuild.
  • Gunpowder is scarce early - Raid Pirate Camps. Save for bosses.
  • Clay is in island center - Dark muddy patches. Stone Pickaxe required.
  • Trade goods can't be opened - Sell at Tortuga traders.
  • Press K to summon your boat - Most important keybind.
🧭
Fast Travel System
How It Actually Works
  • Step 1: Craft or find a Fast Travel Bell (first one free in Smuggler's Den).
  • Step 2: Place at least two points - one origin, one destination. Must be placed in-world.
  • Step 3: A Bonfire must be nearby to enable Wharf/vessel fast travel.
🗺️

Pro Tip: Place bells at every new island and dungeon. No limit. Carry one in inventory for on-the-spot placement.

Master the Soulslite Combat

Stamina management, parry timing, and positioning are everything. Enemies hit hard - brute force will get you killed.

⚔️
Soulslite Combat System
The Core Mechanics
  • Lock on with T - Without lock-on, dodges won't track and guard won't face correctly.
  • Stamina is everything - Every attack, dodge, block drains it. At zero = "winded" (can't sprint or dodge).
  • Parry for stagger - Time block right as attack lands. Better vs humans than animals.
  • Red attacks = unblockable - Dodge these and ground AOE effects.
  • Posture/Shield system - Deplete shield icons below enemy name. Break = stagger.
  • Don't fight +2 level enemies - A Level 3 Sow can one-shot a fresh character.
  • Hunger affects HP directly - Low hunger = declining health. Cooked food grants stat buffs (2 max).
  • Craft bandages early - Coarse Fabric at Workbench. Keep 15-20 on hotbar.
🚨

CRITICAL: Enemy kills give ZERO XP. Combat is for reaching loot, not progression. All XP comes from Journal quests and Site Clear bonuses.

Pirate Camp Efficiency
Clear 40% Faster
  • Strafe to dodge burst - Side-step when you see wind-up. Counter-hit for free.
  • Clear only blocking enemies - Enemies respawn anyway. Kill only those blocking chests.
  • Run past low-threat pirates - If not blocking a chest, just run past and loot.
  • Pull patrol groups one at a time - Isolate at edge of patrol range.
  • Use G-radial to ping enemies - Hold G, select enemy marker. Crew sees it instantly.

Co-op Combat Roles
The 40% Faster Clear Strategy
  • Aggro Holder (1 player) - Engages main enemy group, draws attention. Needs copper/iron weapon.
  • Looters (rest of crew) - Move through camp looting while aggro holder occupies enemies. Light armor + bandages.
📡

Communication: Ping chest clusters with G-radial. All players get full Site Clear XP when last chest is looted.

Building Requirements
Roofs & Materials
  • Armor Workshop - 5 Wood + 10 Coarse Fabric. Requires roof to function!
  • Weaponsmith Workshop - 10 Wood + 5 Copper Ingots. Requires roof to function!
  • Charcoal Kiln - 25 Wood + 20 Clay. 1 Wood → 1 Charcoal.
  • Smelting Furnace - 15 Clay + 30 Stone. 6 Copper Ore + 1 Charcoal → 1 Copper Ingot.
  • Wharf - Unlocked after cannon quest. Repair ship for 20 Wood.
  • Disassembly Table - Break old gear into crafting materials.
⚠️

Buildings cannot be moved after placement. Demolish to refund all materials, then rebuild. Plan your base layout!

Resource Locations
Where to Find Everything
  • Clay - Island center, between Ancient Ruins and Copper Deposit. Dark muddy patches. ~60-80 per deposit.
  • Copper Ore - Caves on islands 1-2. Marked on mini-map during tutorial.
  • Iron Ore - Foothills cave systems. Journal quest guides you there.
  • Steel Components - Cursed Swamps biome.
  • Gunpowder - Pirate Camps (enemy drops + supply boxes). Save for bosses.
  • Plant Fiber - Algae and plants near shore. Use shovel at base of clusters for more.
  • Coarse Fabric - Craft at Workbench from Plant Fiber.

Rule the High Seas

Naval combat, island exploration, and discovering hidden treasures across 30 procedurally generated islands.

🗺️
The Three Biomes
What to Expect
  • Coastal Islands - Starting area. Tropical beaches, basic pirates, boars, crabs. 30 procedurally generated islands.
  • Foothills - Western sector. Higher level enemies, wolves, ancient ruins. Need Iron gear (Lv.8+).
  • Cursed Swamps - Endgame biome. Toughest enemies in EA. Steel gear required.
📍

90+ hand-crafted points of interest across all biomes. Explore thoroughly for unique loot and hidden chests.

⛵
Naval Combat
Ship Management
  • Press K to summon your starter boat anywhere at sea.
  • Ship wrecked? Build a Wharf, pay 20 Wood to repair/salvage, then press K to respawn fully repaired.
  • Loot floats where your ship went down. Don't forget to collect it!
  • Naval combat tip: Maintain circular path around enemy ships to dodge broadsides while keeping cannons aligned. Use heavy cannonballs, then board and clear crew.
📦
Trade Goods & Tortuga
Making Money
  • Sealed boxes (Naval Supplies, Medical Crates, Contraband) cannot be opened - they're trade goods.
  • Sell at Tortuga - Main trading hub unlocked through main quests.
  • Store extras in base chests and haul to Tortuga when you have access.

Israel Hands - The Foothills Tyrant

A multi-phase questline spanning naval warfare, camp infiltration, and a brutal 1v1 duel in the ancient temple.

👑
Israel Hands
Foothills Temple Boss - 1v1 Duel
Difficulty:
Hard

How to Reach Him

1. Start "Needle in a Haystack"

Clear the western pirate camp in Foothills to trigger the questline.

2. Defeat "The Unsinkable"

Naval battle on eastern sea. Circular sailing to dodge broadsides. Board and clear crew after hull is shredded.

3. Search Northwestern Ruins

Avoid wolves. Find the wax-stained desk for the clue.

4. Loot the Windrose Key

From a dead pirate in the northern pirate camp. Key is indestructible and won't be lost on death.

5. Open the Temple

Use key on sealed stone door in Foothills heart. Leads directly to boss arena.

Attack Patterns & Counters

⚔️

Rush Combo (3-4 rapid strikes)

Counter: Parry the final hit - Staggers him, opens high-damage window. Watch for weapon flash.

🔫

Flintlock Shot

Counter: Dodge roll sideways - Do NOT block. Quick projectile from range.

💥

Heavy Slam (Unblockable)

Counter: Backstep or roll behind - Overhead strike with red glow. Can't be blocked.

⚠️

Don't get greedy! Israel Hands has high poise. He'll trade hits if you try more than 3 strikes. Hit 2-3 times, then back off.

Rewards

  • Unique sword - Tailored to your current level
  • Legendary artifact - Passive bonuses (naval speed, parry windows, etc.)
🔄

Key stacks to 100. Some players farm this questline in New Game Plus to optimize artifact rolls.

Co-op Basics
Play With Friends
  • Up to 8 players - But devs recommend 4 max for best performance.
  • Self-hosted and dedicated servers both supported.
  • PvE only - No PvP in current build.
  • Shared Quest Progress - Everyone benefits from main story advancement.
  • Full Site Clear XP for all players when the last chest is looted, regardless of who opened it.
📡
G-Radial Ping System
Crew Coordination
  • Hold G to open radial wheel.
  • Select enemy marker to ping positions for your crew.
  • Use constantly to prevent players walking into uncleared areas blind.
  • Looters should ping chest clusters so aggro holders can position enemies away.
📈
Leveling System
Stats & Talents
  • Attribute Points (3 stats):
  • • Strength - Determines effective weapon usage
  • • Health - Max HP increase
  • • Stamina - Fight longer before going winded
  • Talents (passive upgrades, 1 per level):
  • • +20% Stamina | Improved Slash Damage | Better Crit Chance | Damage Resistance | Marathon Runner
💪

New player advice: Put early points into Stamina or Health. Food buffs and Comfort Level also boost effective stats.
